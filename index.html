About Me

The web has always captured my interest. I remember wondering how the pages got onto the internet and how we can't edit them like we do a Word or Excel sheet. That is how my journey in web development began, a level of curiosity that could not be satiated until I had to be part of it.

Two years later, I have the same level of curiosity backed by experience. I have recently built a GBV support platform and a Grants & Events scraper.
